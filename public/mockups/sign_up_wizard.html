What is Kroogi
If you’re an artist, chances are that your music is already being distributed over the internet for free.
Kroogi gives you the opportunity to get some compensation for offering free downloads - and, in the process, reach more fans all over the world. So, if you’re bored with MySpace, and looking for a new way to share your music, join Kroogi!
  • Get Distributed: it’s free to create a Kroogi profile, where you can upload your music, art, photos, and videos. Your fans can download your content, and you’ll receive contributions from downloads through PayPal and other payment systems.
  • Get Paid: let your fans make contributions to you before they download, after they download or with no downloads at all. Although free downloads is an important part of our site, most listeners,will pay for their music if given a choice
  • Get Promoted: You and your fans can share/play your albums on any webpage (such as Myspace, Livejournal, blogs, etc), using our Flash widget. And, Your music albums will be available for downloading and distribution on Facebook via our KroogiDownloads application.
  • Get Recognized: Kroogi’s purpose is to help artists to gain recognition throughout the world and allow fans from anywhere to contribute what they can.
If you’re a fan, Kroogi is the place for you.
  • Find New Art: Discover new interesting music, visual art and videos from different parts of the world.
  • Interact and Support: Kroogi Artists are very hand- on and they would love to hear from you! You can support artists through contributions, reviews, and by giving feedback.
  • Promote and Inspire: Help others to learn about music and support the artists you you love . Share your favorite music using our embeddable player, or Facebook app, and receive credit for your effort from artist and Kroogi.
