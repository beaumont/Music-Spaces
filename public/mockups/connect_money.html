This is how receiving contributions works for artists and projects on Kroogi.com
1. Artist or project have to be verified first. They should attach PayPal account or Webmoney Z-purse (USD) before they can start receiving any contributions .
2. Once PayPal or Webmoney account is attached and verified, artist or project can receive contributions via following ways: during music album download, during folder content download, via contributions area on Kroogi page.
3. When artist or project add Music Album to their Kroogi page and make it downloadable, it will immediately be available on Kroogi Downloads Facebook application. Facebook users can distribute the album among themselves, download the album and make contributions via PayPal.
4. Overall, every Kroogi artist or project will be able to receive donations via PayPal, Webmoney (USD, EUR, RUR), and SMS (selected countries). This contributions will be collected and held on Kroogi account in artist's or project's name.
5. Artist or project will be able to withdraw received contributions to their attached PayPal or Webmoney (USD) account upon 30 days (holding period is subject to change).
6. To receive contributions artist or project must agree to service fee withheld by Kroogi: 15% from all contributions.
